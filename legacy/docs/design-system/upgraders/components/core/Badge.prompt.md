Tinted status pill — campaign states, priorities, levels. Tone sets colour; `dot` adds a live indicator; `solid` is the loud red variant.

```jsx
<Badge tone="warning" dot>En cours</Badge>
<Badge tone="success" dot>Terminé</Badge>
<Badge tone="info" dot>En attente</Badge>
<Badge tone="danger" solid>Priorité haute</Badge>
<Badge tone="level" icon={<Star />}>Level 7</Badge>
<Badge tone="success">Active</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral · accent · success · warning · danger · info · level`.
